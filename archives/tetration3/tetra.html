Tetration

Tetration

Definitions

  • Tetration is an iterational of an exponential from 1.
  • A tetrate function is tetration with a constant height.
  • A tetrational function is tetration with a constant base.
  • A super-root is an inverse function of a tetrate function.
  • A super-log is an inverse function of a tetrational function.

Notation

There are two related tetration notations:

(Maurer) for tetration
(Euler) for iterated exponentials
