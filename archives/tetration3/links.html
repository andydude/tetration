Links

These are a few links to other tetration-related websites.

Tetration Websites

  • (B1)
    		•  Jonathan Bowers Array Notation, which provides an alternative extension of hyper-operators to more than 3 parameters.
  • (G1)
    		•  Ioannis N. Galidakis Mathematics, which includes many papers detailing tetration and related functions, such as an extension of the Lambert W-function to the quaternions.
  • (G2)
    		•  Daniel Geisler Tetration.org, which includes several Mathematica notebooks, and many pages discussing the dynamical and combinatorial aspects of tetration.
  • (M1)
    		•  Robert Munafo Large Numbers, which lists several hundred named large numbers, and has a few articles which discuss the basics of tetration.
  • (S1)
    		•  Neil J. A. Sloane Encyclopedia of Integer Sequences, which has several dozen sequences directly related to tetration, such as A000258.
  • (T1)
    		•  Henryk Trappmann Tetration Forum, which provides an outlet for people to talk about tetration, and an archive of many, many insightful discussions.
  • (W1)
    		•  Eric W. Weisstein,
    Ioannis N. Galidakis         		MathWorld's Power Tower page, which includes many references, series expansions and complex plots of special cases of tetraiton.
  • (W2)
    		•  Various Wikipedia's Tetration page, which is constantly being updated with recently published information.
